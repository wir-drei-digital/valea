# Discovery Call

A free, 30-minute call to explore whether coaching is a good fit — no preparation required, no obligation to continue.

## What it covers

- what the person is currently working through
- whether the Founder Coaching Package fits their situation
- how sessions work and what to expect

## Not a fit

- crisis situations needing immediate support
- anything better addressed by a medical or legal professional

## Preferred next step

If it's a fit, propose the Founder Coaching Package. If it's unclear, offer another short conversation instead of pushing to book.
